### material shortcuts

1+Left Click = Constant Scalar
3+Left Click = Constant 3 for color / Right Click the node to convert to parameter
S+Left Click = Scalar Parameter
L+Left Click = LERP
M+Left Click = Multiply
U+Left Click = TextCoord Node
